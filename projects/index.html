Mano-a-many

 (source code)

Mano-a-Many helps friends stay friends when they find themselves needing to share a bill at a restaurant or pick up coffee for the office. Users can keep track of what they owe their friends, and how much their friends owe them. For larger groups, users can consolidate everything down to a few transactions.

We had five-days to complete a challenging implementation of some complex mathematics. The site needed a simple interface that helped users understand how we arrived at the solution. I wrote most of the front-end including AJAX calls to make the UX as smooth as possible.
